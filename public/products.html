Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. What’s the price of the Australia Trip Package from India?

Prices vary depending on the package chosen, but they generally start from around ₹ 50,000.

Q. What’s the best time to visit Australia?

The best time to visit Australia is during spring (September to November) and autumn (March to May) when the weather is pleasant.

Q. What are the best places to visit in Australia?

Some of the best places include Sydney, Melbourne, Great Barrier Reef, Gold Coast, and Uluru.

Q. Do Indian citizens require a Visa to visit Australia?

Yes, Indian citizens need a visa to enter Australia.

Q. Which Airlines fly to Australia from India?

Airlines like Qantas, Air India, and Singapore Airlines offer flights from India to Australia.

Q. What’s the average flight duration between India to Australia?

The average flight duration is approximately 12-14 hours, depending on the route.
